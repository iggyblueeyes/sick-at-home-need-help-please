Lab-evaluated Tests

Do you need a ride to the nearest Covid testing center?

We'll schedule the appointment for you and tell you when you need to be ready for pick up.

How many others want to come along to get tested?

We have space for up to 3 others maximum that ride with you. Everyone that comes must get tested. We recommend only those that have symptoms to come. We don't want to exposed anyone that aren't displaying symptoms.

Rapid Tests

How many rapid tests do you need?

We recommend that everyone you might have exposed get tested one way or another. If they don't have symptoms, we recommend that they find their own way to a Covid testing center or use one of our rapid tests.
