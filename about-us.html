About Us

We're a service-focused tech startup based in the Greater Phoenix Area.

We're serving the community by providing access to supplies and testing to those suffering with Covid symptoms at home. We'll even talk them to a Covid testing center if they can't get there by themselves.

We are limiting our focus to the City of Tempe, and we plan to expand to the whole of the Greater Phoenix Area: Mesa, Phoenix, Gilbert, Chandler, Scottsdale, Scottsdale, Glendale, ect. We will expand as soon as we are confident that we can provide quality service. We'll be in your city sooner than you think!

Our founder is a grad school drop out and is on staff at a K-8th school in Phoenix. Originally from Tennessee, he's lived in the Greater Phoenix Area for 4+ years. He loves the desert and the desert people.

We'll put more about our story here as it evolves. Stay tuned!
