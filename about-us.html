About us

Startup in Tempe, AZ

We're a service-focused startup based in Tempe, AZ.

We're serving the community by providing access to supplies and testing to those suffering with Covid symptoms at home. We'll even talk them to a Covid testing center if they can't get there by themselves.

We are limiting our focus to the City of Tempe, and we plan to expand to the whole of the Greater Phoenix Area. We will expand as soon as we are confident that we can provide quality service. We'll be in your area sooner than you think!

We'll put more about our story here as it evolves. Stay tuned!
