Careers

Service provider

Description of duties

You deliver supplies, rapid tests, and drive clients to/from testing centers.

Requirements

You have background in customer service, care giving, or nursing. You're professional, timely, and want to help people in need. You communicate well, both to teammates and clients.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I test positive?

Serving people with Covid symptoms puts you at risk. That's why we put a lot of thought into our sick leave policy. We care about our service providers as much as our customers. Given a positive test, we have a 7-day quarantine policy for all of our service providers. We'll pay you while you're sick. We pay you the amount you earned over the last 7 days.

How do I get paid?

You'll come on board with us as a 1099 independent contractor. You get paid every time you service a customer. The details are subject to change as we figure out our business model. Your interviewer will dicusss this in detail with you. And know that you are the most important part of our operation and we want to do well by you.

Do I get benefits?

As of right now we are not offering benefits. We are too small to do so. Ideally you have benefits through another organization. We hope to soon offer benefits as we grow and serve more people in need.
